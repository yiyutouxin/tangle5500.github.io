# 演示

项目 <https://github.com/zenorocha/clipboard.js>

```
<script type="text/javascript" src="dist/clipboard.min.js"></script>
<button class="copy" data-clipboard-text="copy text">复制到剪切板</button>
<script>new ClipboardJS('.copy');</script>
```
