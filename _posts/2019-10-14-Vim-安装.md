# 下载地址

https://github.com/vim/vim-win32-installer/releases/tag/v8.0.0003

把下面两个文件复制到

```text
gvim_8.0.0003_x86\vim80\ctags.exe
gvim_8.0.0003_x86\vim80\cscope.exe
```

根目录添加

```text
vimfiles
_vimrc
dict.txt
gvimfullscreen.dll
```
