打开 **https://highlightjs.org/download/** 选择语言包并下载
然后引用

```html
<link rel="stylesheet" href="/monokai-sublime.css">
<script src="/highlight.min.js"></script>
<script>hljs.initHighlightingOnLoad();</script>
```
