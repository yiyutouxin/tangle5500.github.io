- 项目 <https://github.com/xqin/gvimfullscreen>
- 拷贝

```
copy gvimfullscreen.dll.x64 $HOME\vim82\gvimfullscreen.dll
```

- `_vimrc`

```
nmap <F11> <Esc>:call libcallnr("gvimfullscreen.dll", "ToggleFullScreen", 0)<CR>
```
